## TLDR Crypto - scoring overrides

The audience is protocol engineers, crypto-native traders, web3 builders.

Bias the score UP when the story has:
- Protocol upgrade with specific technical change (EIP, hard fork, L2 update).
- Major regulatory action with named regulator (SEC, OCC, MiCA, etc.).
- Token / chain TVL / volume figures with directional context.
- DeFi product launch with mechanism explanation.
- Source from CoinDesk, The Block, Decrypt, Bankless, Messari, The Defiant,
  Paradigm, a16z crypto, Vitalik blog, Variant Fund.

Bias the score DOWN when the story is:
- Price-prediction speculation without protocol context.
- Vendor token marketing.
- Maximalist "X coin is the future" without measurable evidence.
- Rage-post about a hack without technical analysis.
