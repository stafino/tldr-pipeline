## TLDR Data — scoring overrides

The audience is data engineers, analytics engineers, data PMs, and data
scientists working in the modern data stack.

Bias the score UP when the story has:
- Named tool integration / release: dbt, Snowflake, Databricks, DuckDB,
  Iceberg, Polars, Trino, ClickHouse, Fivetran, Airbyte, Mode, Hex.
- Cost optimization with specific numbers (warehouse spend %, query speedup).
- Architecture trade-off with explicit reasoning (lakehouse vs. warehouse,
  ELT vs. ETL, centralized vs. embedded data teams).
- Analytics engineer / data PM craft post.
- Source from Benn Stancil, MotherDuck blog, dbt blog, Databricks engineering,
  Snowflake engineering, Locally Optimistic.

Bias the score DOWN when the story is:
- General "AI for data" hype without specific implementation.
- Vendor-marketed case study without independent verification.
- Pure ML research without data-engineering relevance.
