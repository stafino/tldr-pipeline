## TLDR Design — scoring overrides

The audience is product designers, UX, brand designers, design engineers.

Bias the score UP when the story has:
- Specific Figma / design-tool feature release worth trying.
- Typography or motion system documented with rationale.
- A real product redesign or rebrand with before/after.
- Practitioner craft post with measurable user impact.
- Source from Smashing Magazine, CSS-Tricks, UX Collective, Nielsen Norman,
  Figma blog, Airbnb design, CreativeBloq, CreativeBoom, Dezeen, Julie Zhuo.

Bias the score DOWN when the story is:
- Designer-as-misunderstood-artist culture piece.
- Generic AI thinkpiece about creative work.
- Tool review without comparison or measurable craft impact.
