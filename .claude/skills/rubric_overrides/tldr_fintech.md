## TLDR Fintech — scoring overrides

The audience is payments engineers, banking-infra builders, fintech operators.

Bias the score UP when the story has:
- Payment rail update (FedNow, RTP, ACH, Visa Direct).
- Banking-as-a-Service / sponsor-bank news with named institution.
- Stablecoin / cross-border payment infrastructure.
- Regulatory action with specific regulator (OCC, FDIC, CFPB, FCA, BaFin).
- Source from Sifted, Fintech Takes, Net Interest, Finextra, The Block (fintech).

Bias the score DOWN when the story is:
- Generic "banks vs. neobanks" think piece without mechanism.
- Marketing-led fintech press release.
- Bitcoin price commentary without fintech infrastructure angle.
