## TLDR IT - scoring overrides

The audience is enterprise IT leaders, CIOs, infrastructure architects.

Bias the score UP when the story has:
- Major enterprise vendor move (Microsoft, Oracle, IBM, SAP, ServiceNow,
  Workday, Salesforce, Cisco).
- Licensing / pricing change affecting Fortune 500 procurement.
- AWS / Azure / GCP product announcement with enterprise relevance.
- Workspace / collaboration / identity tooling shift.
- Source from CIODive, CIO, InfoWorld, BleepingComputer (enterprise angle),
  TheRegister, Microsoft blog, Workspace Updates Google blog, SDXCentral.

Bias the score DOWN when the story is:
- Developer-tooling story not relevant to enterprise IT operations.
- Consumer-facing tech without enterprise procurement implication.
- AI hype without enterprise deployment angle.
