## TLDR Marketing - scoring overrides

The audience is growth/demand-gen/brand marketers. They read for channel
shifts and specific tactics tested with real numbers.

Bias the score UP when the story has:
- Named channel benchmark or CAC/conversion number (CTR lift, channel-mix
  shift, organic-search impact percentages).
- AI-search / LLM-overview impact on brand visibility (high-priority topic).
- Practical SEO/SEM/social tactics with a tested protocol.
- Source = MarketingBrew, MarketingDive, SearchEngineLand, MarTech,
  ConversionRateExperts, NeilPatel, GrowthMemo, SocialMediaToday,
  thestateofbrand, Foundation Inc, AnnHandley, Animalz.
- Platform changes from Google (Ads, AIO), Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok that
  affect demand-gen.

Bias the score DOWN when the story is:
- Generic "AI in marketing" prediction piece without tactics.
- Brand-positioning philosophy without applied example.
- Agency-thought-leadership content marketing.
- Recruiting / hiring news without measurable conversion impact.
