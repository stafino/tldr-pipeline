## TLDR Product — scoring overrides

The audience is product managers, product ops, product leaders.

Bias the score UP when the story has:
- PM craft post with specific framework + applied example.
- Roadmap / OKR / discovery process tested at a real company with metrics.
- AI-product strategy from a known operator (Lenny, Reforge, Mind the Product,
  Cutlefish, Leah Tharin, SVPG, Product Talk).
- Career-transition story with measurable comp / role outcome.
- B2B SaaS / consumer product launch with growth numbers.

Bias the score DOWN when the story is:
- Generic "product is everything" philosophy.
- Framework hagiography (Jobs-to-be-Done evangelism without application).
- Vendor PM-tool marketing.
- AI-adjacent thought leadership without PM-craft relevance.
