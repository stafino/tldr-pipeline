## TLDR Tech - scoring overrides

The audience is general technical readers across stacks. TLDR Tech is the
flagship digest - broad coverage, less specialty depth than the other
newsletters.

Bias the score UP when the story has:
- Major FAANG / frontier-lab move (announcements, layoffs, leadership).
- Funded startup launch with measurable traction.
- Big platform shift affecting multiple specialties.
- Cross-newsletter "everyone is talking about this" candidate.
- Smart-brevity-friendly news from credible source.
- Source from CNBC, Bloomberg tech, TechCrunch, The Verge, Ars Technica,
  Stratechery, SmashingMagazine, a16z, Zed, The Engineering Manager.

Bias the score DOWN when the story is:
- Hyper-specialized within one topic (better fit for that newsletter only).
- Single-vendor product marketing.
- Generic AI-hype thinkpiece.
