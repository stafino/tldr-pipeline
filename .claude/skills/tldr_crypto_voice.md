# TLDR Crypto — Voice Addendum

Audience: protocol engineers, crypto traders, web3 founders, infra researchers. Read for protocol updates, market structure, and regulatory action. Voice is neutral and technical — not shill, not anti-crypto either.

## Length and shape

- **2 sentences, 40-80 words.** Guides/Resources up to 90. Quick Links: 1 sentence.

## Audience-targeting

- Use protocol names directly (Ethereum, Solana, Base, Optimism, Arbitrum, Sui, Bittensor). Use the L2-rollup-specific terminology.
- Numbers matter: TVL, daily active addresses, fee burned, treasury size, validator count.
- Skip both maximalist framing ("Bitcoin is the future of money") and dismissive framing ("crypto is dead"). State what happened.

## Examples

> **The SEC dropped its case against Coinbase, ending a two-year overhang on the listed exchange and clarifying that secondary-market token sales of registered exchange-listed assets aren't securities transactions by default.** The ruling reopens a path for US-based protocols to list without going offshore first.

> **Ethereum's Pectra upgrade activated on mainnet, raising the validator effective balance ceiling from 32 to 2,048 ETH and consolidating reward distribution.** The change reduces the validator-set overhead by ~80% over time, helping nodes that operate large staking pools.

> **Solana's transaction count overtook Ethereum L1 + all major L2s combined for the first time on a non-airdrop day.** A growing share of the activity is real-time consumer applications using compressed NFTs and Anchor program optimizations.

## Section nuance

- **📈 Markets & Business:** Funding, exchange news, ETF flows, regulatory actions.
- **🚀 Innovation & Launches:** Protocol launches, L2 upgrades, new chains, DeFi product releases.
- **💡 Guides & Resources:** Tutorials, deep dives into a protocol mechanism.
- **🦄 Miscellaneous:** Hiring, exec moves, conferences.
- **⚡ Quick Links:** One sentence.

## What's distinctive

- Neutral technical register. No "to the moon", no "rug pull" as casual modifier.
- Use protocol/chain names precisely.
- Skip price-prediction takes; report what's measurable.
