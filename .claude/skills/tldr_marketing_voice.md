# TLDR Marketing — Voice Addendum

Audience: growth marketers, demand-gen leads, brand marketers, content strategists. Read for channel shifts, campaign playbooks, and AI's impact on marketing work.

## Length and shape

- **2 sentences, 40-80 words.** Strategies/Tactics can hit 100. Quick Links: 1 sentence.

## Audience-targeting

- Use channel-specific vocabulary: CAC, payback period, MQL→SQL conversion, CTR, ROAS, organic share-of-voice, dark-social attribution.
- Name platforms (Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn Ads, TikTok, HubSpot, Marketo). Skip "modern marketing tech stack" — say which one.
- Numbers carry. Channel benchmarks, CAC changes, conversion lifts get a number when available.

## Examples

> **Google's AI Overviews now appear on roughly 35% of informational searches, and B2B SaaS sites are reporting 20-30% drops in long-tail organic traffic as AI answers absorb the click.** Teams that have started preserving traffic are publishing more bottom-of-funnel pages with strong proprietary data and de-emphasizing top-of-funnel SEO.

> **Around 85% of B2B prospects prefer chat over voice for AI sales interactions, and chat is also cheaper to deploy and monitor.** The teams seeing results use AI for top-of-funnel qualification and triage, then hand to a human the moment the conversation needs nuance.

> **HubSpot acquired Frame.ai to bring conversation analytics into the customer platform, giving marketers structured signal from sales calls and support chats.** The play is to close the loop between marketing-attributed pipeline and the actual conversations that determined whether a deal closed.

## Section nuance

- **🤳 News & Trends:** Platform changes, ad-policy moves, AI-search shifts.
- **🚀 Strategies & Tactics:** Channel playbooks, campaign post-mortems, benchmark reports.
- **🧑‍💻 Resources & Tools:** New marketing tools, dashboards, templates.
- **🎁 Miscellaneous:** Agency moves, hiring, brand-strategy essays.
- **⚡ Quick Links:** One sentence.

## What's distinctive

- Tactical specificity (channel, CAC, conversion) matters.
- Less product-engineering than TLDR Product.
- Voice respects practitioners who are accountable for a number this quarter.
