Wayfinder Router: deterministic routing of queries between local and hosted LLM (1 minute read)

Wayfinder Router is a deterministic prompt-complexity router that decides whether to send each query to a local or hosted LLM by reading the prompt's structure and wording, offline and in microseconds, without calling another model to make the call. It composes with provider gateways like OpenRouter or LiteLLM, handling the cheap-versus-expensive tier decision while they handle which provider serves the call.
