SEC Move Could Bolster Tokenized Stocks (1 minute read)

SEC Commissioner Paul Atkins signaled possible changes to Rule 611, the order-protection rule that governs how trades route to the best available price, in comments that Bloomberg Intelligence reads as favorable to tokenized equities. Loosening the rule could let tokenized stocks trade on blockchain-based venues without routing to incumbent exchanges, removing a structural barrier that has kept on-chain equity products marginal.

BlackRock Launches New Bitcoin Income ETF (1 minute read)

BlackRock launched the iShares Bitcoin Premium Income ETF under ticker BITA, a covered-call product that sells options against spot bitcoin exposure to generate yield. The fund extends BlackRock's digital asset lineup beyond its spot IBIT vehicle, targeting investors who want bitcoin exposure paired with regular income distributions rather than pure price appreciation.

U.S. senators urge Treasury not to leave states out of GENIUS Act stablecoin process (5 minute read)

A bipartisan group of senators led by Cynthia Lummis pressed the Treasury to give states an explicit certification process under the GENIUS Act, arguing its proposed principles omitted the timeline and procedural steps states need to prove their stablecoin oversight matches federal standards. Without clear guidance, the lawmakers warned, the certification process could be applied in a way that forecloses state-level participation entirely.

The Ghosts of Polymarket: When Off-Chain Matches Meet On-Chain Reverts (1 minute read)

This paper analyzes "Ghost Fills" on Polymarket, the consistency gap created by its hybrid architecture that matches orders off-chain but settles them on-chain. The authors built GHOSTHUNTER to reconstruct failed settlements from on-chain traces, examining 1,952,440 reverted match-order transactions where attackers exploited the time gap to invalidate already-matched orders before finalization. They identify four attack vectors—nonce bump, balance drain, allowance revoke, and proxy trap—realized across 35 evolving variants.
