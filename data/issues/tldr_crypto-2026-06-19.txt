CryptoQuant says bitcoin microtransactions now make up 80% of daily transactions (1 minute read)

Bitcoin transactions below 0.01 BTC now account for roughly 80% of daily transactions, up from about 44% in 2023, pushing network activity to its highest level since late 2024 even as prices stay weak, according to CryptoQuant. The firm attributes the surge to OP_RETURN data inscriptions from Runes, Ordinals, and BRC-20 protocols, which have driven the mempool to 128,000 unconfirmed transactions and could raise fees for time-sensitive transfers.
