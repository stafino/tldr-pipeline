Ethereum validators asked to fund projects with up to 10% of staking rewards under new proposal (5 minute read)

A new Ethereum governance proposal called "validator redirected revenue" would let validators redirect 0% to 10% of their staking rewards toward shared ecosystem infrastructure and public goods, with funds routed through a splitter contract. If a majority of validators signal support for a nonzero rate, the contribution becomes mandatory for all, potentially channeling tens of thousands of ETH annually into projects that address the network's free-rider problem.

Bitcoin developers want to fix the 'replace this transaction with a higher fee' button. Here's why (5 minute read)

Bitcoin Core developers are proposing to remove explicit replace-by-fee (RBF) signaling from wallet software, since full-RBF is now the network's default policy and the opt-in flag has become redundant. Keeping the legacy signal leaves an on-chain fingerprint that reveals which wallet sent a transaction, so developers are coordinating on a shared default sequence number, likely the already dominant MAX-2, to make transactions across wallets indistinguishable.

Bitget enables direct US stock purchases using crypto (1 minute read)

Bitget launched Stock+, a feature that lets users buy full and fractional US stocks funded directly with crypto through regulated brokers. The product routes orders to licensed brokerages, bringing equity exposure onto the exchange without requiring users to convert holdings to fiat first.

South Korea’s Toss Bank to test blockchain-based financial infrastructure on Solana (1 minute read)

Toss Bank, South Korea's internet-only bank with 15 million customers, signed an MOU with the Solana Foundation to build blockchain financial infrastructure, starting with a proof-of-concept for stablecoin-based overseas remittances and settlement. The first phase verifies technical feasibility of Solana-powered transfers before later phases add overseas partners and AML/KYC checks across Toss's existing 30-country, seven-currency remittance network.
