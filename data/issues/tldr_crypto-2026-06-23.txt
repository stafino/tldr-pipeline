OptChain: Achieving Optimal Throughput of Permissionless Blockchains (1 minute read)

OptChain is a permissionless blockchain SMR protocol that the authors claim is the first to approach a theoretical upper bound on throughput under fixed error probability. It combines Shardis, a verifiable information dispersal mechanism that pushes intra-shard throughput to the fastest node's bandwidth limit, with diffusion mining, which keeps each shard secure as long as it holds one honest node. The paper backs the design with a formal security analysis and an AWS EC2 prototype that outperforms existing permissionless protocols.
