Coinbase-incubated Base suffers mainnet chain stall interrupting block production (1 minute read)

Deposits, withdrawals, and transactions were delayed or stalled, and the incident landed the same day as Base's scheduled Beryl upgrade, though it appears unrelated to the planned maintenance.

Uniswap, Spark aim to build stablecoin FX market as banks, fintechs enter the industry (5 minute read)

Spark and Uniswap are building a shared "FX layer" for stablecoins, a cross-issuer liquidity network that lets capital move between tokens and earn yield while idle, starting with a $150M migration of USDS, USDT, and PYUSD liquidity to Uniswap v4. The bet is that as bank- and fintech-issued stablecoins multiply, the liquidity routing layer between issuers becomes the contested infrastructure rather than the tokens themselves.
