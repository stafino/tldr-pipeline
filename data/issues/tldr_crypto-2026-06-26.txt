Bipartisan senators call for CFTC investigation into Polymarket after fake bets report (1 minute read)

Senators John Curtis (R-Utah) and Adam Schiff (D-Calif.) asked the CFTC to investigate Polymarket after a Wall Street Journal report found the platform paid creators to stage fake bets, including faked wins on replica sites across 1,105 videos worth $1.9M in unreal wagers. The bipartisan letter questions whether the agency can regulate prediction markets, a sector that has ballooned alongside Polymarket's $15B valuation.

Spain says ‘no exceptions or extensions’ for Binance, other crypto firms ahead of MiCA deadline (1 minute read)

Spain's securities regulator, the CNMV, said it will grant no exceptions or extensions to Binance and other crypto firms that miss the December 30 deadline to obtain MiCA authorization. Firms operating without a license after the cutoff must cease services to Spanish customers, ending the transitional grandfathering period that let unregistered providers continue under prior national rules.

Binance Tells EU Users It Will Wind Down Services as MiCA Deadline Hits (1 minute read)

Binance has begun telling EU users in France, Italy, Poland, and Spain that it will wind down services after failing to secure a MiCA license before the July 1 deadline, halting new registrations and restricting access. The exchange withdrew its Greek application Wednesday and says it will pursue authorization in another member state, joining the roughly 83% of EU crypto providers still unlicensed under the framework.
