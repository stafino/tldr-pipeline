$4 billion gone. Spot bitcoin ETFs are on track for their worst month on record (5 minute read)

US-listed spot bitcoin ETFs have logged $4.06 billion in net outflows in June, the largest monthly redemption since the products launched in January 2024 and exceeding the prior $3.56 billion record set in February 2025. The exodus, which follows $2.43 billion of May redemptions, tracks bitcoin's roughly 30% first-half decline as institutional demand reverses despite earlier hopes of a post-SpaceX-IPO bid.

SBI's $289 million Bitbank deal is symptomatic of Japan's crypto consolidation: Architect Partners (5 minute read)

SBI Holdings acquired Japanese crypto exchange Bitbank for $289 million, doubling its assets under custody to roughly 1.1 trillion yen ($6.8B) and combined accounts to 2.9 million as tougher Japanese rules raise the cost of running standalone exchanges. Investment bank Architect Partners reads the deal as a bet on regulated scale, naming private-equity-owned bitFlyer as the next likely consolidation target.
