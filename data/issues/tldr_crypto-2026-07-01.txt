Tokenized Google stock inflated 7,700% in rare DeFi lending exploit (5 minute read)

Edel Finance halted its lending protocol after an attacker manipulated the exchange rate between wGOOGLx and its underlying tokenized Google share GOOGLx, inflating the wrapped collateral to roughly 78 times its true value and borrowing against it for about $403,000 in bad debt. Chainlink oracles reported Alphabet's ~$357 share price correctly, so the flaw was in the wrapping mechanism, not the price feed; Edel will absorb depositor losses and ship a redesigned v2.

Ripple, Coinbase among top donors in crypto’s $189 million election spending: report (1 minute read)

Crypto companies have contributed $189 million to the 2026 US midterms so far, or 37% of all disclosed corporate political spending, with Ripple ($49.6M), Crypto.com ($38.6M), and Coinbase ($35.2M) leading the sector, according to a Public Citizen analysis of FEC data. Most of the money flowed to the crypto-focused Fairshake super PAC, which took $82.6 million, and the Trump-backing MAGA Inc., which took $56.2 million.
