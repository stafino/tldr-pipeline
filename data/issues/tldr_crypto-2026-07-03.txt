IMF says policy choices will determine whether tokenization strengthens or fragments the financial system (1 minute read)

The IMF's Tobias Adrian argued in a blog post that policy decisions on money, market infrastructure, and legal frameworks will determine whether tokenization strengthens or fragments the financial system as assets move onto shared ledgers. He warned the shift could concentrate risk in platforms, code, and infrastructure providers rather than intermediary balance sheets, and identified tokenized deposits, stablecoins, and tokenized central bank reserves as the three emerging settlement assets.
