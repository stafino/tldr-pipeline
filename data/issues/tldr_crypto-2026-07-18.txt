OKX Launches Tokenized US Stocks on Shared Order Book (1 minute read)

Each ticker is backed 1:1 by shares held in custody via Backed's xStocks and routes every issuer's version into a single order book, OKX's pitch against the fragmented per-issuer markets on Kraken and Binance.

AI Meets Cryptography 2: What AI Found in OpenVM's ZkVM (1 minute read)

zkSecurity's second post in its AI-auditing series points its zkao agent at OpenVM's zkVM, where the tool surfaced a critical soundness bug in the openvm-pairing guest library that let a malicious prover forge any pairing equality. The flaw, assigned CVE-2026-46669 and fixed in OpenVM 1.6.0, affected only code using the vulnerable library, not the proving system itself. The writeup details why naive LLM setups fail on densely coupled zkVM code and how the team validated the AI's candidate finding before disclosure.
