Hyperliquid to Open HIP-4 Prediction Markets Behind 500,000 HYPE Stake (1 minute read)

Hyperliquid said a future HIP-4 enhancement will let anyone deploy permissionless prediction markets by staking 500,000 HYPE, roughly $30M, which validators can slash for markets that are poorly defined or settled incorrectly. Deployers earn up to 50% of trading fees and settle every outstanding market before withdrawing, pushing Hyperliquid deeper into a sector dominated by Polymarket and Kalshi.

Inside Cardano's 'Van Rossum' hard fork, and what it means for users (5 minute read)

Cardano activated its Van Rossem hard fork on July 18, moving the mainnet to protocol version 11, lowering smart contract execution costs and laying groundwork for the planned Ouroboros Leios scaling upgrade. It is the first upgrade in Cardano's history proposed, debated, and ratified entirely through the network's onchain governance system rather than by founding engineering firm Input Output.
