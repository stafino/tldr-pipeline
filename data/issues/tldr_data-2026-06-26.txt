pgEdge joins rush to merge OLTP and OLAP storage to support AI (2 minute read)

pgEdge introduced a beta of ColdFront, a PostgreSQL-native tiering layer that automatically moves older data into Apache Iceberg object storage while keeping PostgreSQL as the only interface applications touch, with DuckDB executing queries against the cold tier. It joins Databricks LTAP, EDB's converged analytics, and Snowflake's pg_lake in collapsing OLTP and OLAP, but differs by treating Iceberg as a transparent storage tier behind Postgres rather than a separate analytical engine.
