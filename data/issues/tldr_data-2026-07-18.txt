Learning a few things about running SQLite (1 minute read)

Julia Evans documents operating SQLite as a real production database for a Django site, learning that "SQLite is fine for small sites" glosses over the operational work databases still demand. Running ANALYZE cut a 4000-row FTS5 query from 5 seconds to 0.05 by giving the planner statistics to work with. The sharper lesson is concurrency: a slow bulk DELETE held a write lock long enough for another worker to hit its 5-second timeout and crash the VM.
