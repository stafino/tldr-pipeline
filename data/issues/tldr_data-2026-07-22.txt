NetApp snaps up DataPelago to bolster AI data infrastructure portfolio (1 minute read)

NetApp acquired DataPelago, a 2021-founded startup whose Nucleus engine uses GPU and CPU acceleration to process data where it sits, and will embed that engine directly in its storage layer. DataPelago claims up to 80% lower infrastructure costs and 10x faster processing versus copying data into a separate compute environment, which puts NetApp in the same push-compute-to-storage territory as Iceberg-on-object-storage query engines.

Fivetran + dbt Labs: The future of dbt Core v2.0 (1 minute read)

dbt Labs released dbt Core v2.0 under an Apache 2.0 license, making the same engine that powers the dbt platform open source a year after the Fusion engine announcement and the Fivetran merger. The release ships alongside dbt State, a caching layer the company says cuts dbt-driven compute by more than 30%, and dbt Wizard, a dbt-specific coding agent.
