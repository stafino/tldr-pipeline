Adobe embeds agentic AI workflows across Creative Cloud, shifting from media generation to production orchestration (2 minute read)

Adobe moved its Creative Agent into public beta across Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Frame.io, repositioning its AI from media generator to an orchestration layer that reads natural-language prompts and drives each app's APIs to run multi-step production tasks. The upgraded Firefly studio adds Elements, a visual variables library for reusing characters and objects across generations, and Projects, a persistent memory layer that retains assets and session context.

Anthropic ships major Claude Design overhaul with design system imports, code round-trips, and a fix for its token-burning problem (2 minute read)

Anthropic overhauled Claude Design, adding design-system imports from GitHub repos or design files, code round-trips, and a rebuilt editor that cuts the heavy token consumption that let early users exhaust a weekly Claude Pro allowance in under half an hour. The update repositions the tool from a prototyping demo into a brand-compliance layer that checks generated UI against imported components and auto-corrects before export.

Snap Specs bring rich augmented reality to smart glasses (1 minute read)

Snap released the Specs, standalone AR glasses that combine the immersive display of a mixed-reality headset with the lightweight frame of consumer AI glasses, shown this week at the Augmented World Expo. The product targets a middle ground between bulky passthrough headsets and display-less audio glasses, betting that designers and developers will build for a form factor light enough to wear in public.

Adobe expands Firefly capabilities, extends agentic tools to Creative Cloud apps (1 minute read)

Adobe expanded Firefly with pre-built Creative Skills, including a Brand Kit creation flow that generates logos, palettes, fonts, and social-channel promo videos from a single prompt, plus new Elements and Projects features for reusing characters and organizing assets. The company also added agentic Firefly sidebars to Premiere, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Frame.io, letting users delegate tasks like renaming assets, fact-checking against briefs, and applying brand-guideline updates.

Heatherwick Studio unveils "space observatory" in AlUla (1 minute read)

Heatherwick Studio released designs for AlUla Manara, a textured-stone stargazing and research center planned for the Saudi desert near the AlUla UNESCO World Heritage Site. The building is shaped around the region's exceptional night-sky visibility, pairing a public visitor experience with astronomical research facilities in one of the few remaining low-light-pollution sites accessible to a mass audience.

Midjourney, the AI image generator, is developing a full-body ultrasonic scanner (1 minute read)

Midjourney, best known for its text-to-image AI, announced Midjourney Medical and its first hardware product: a scanner that submerges you in water and uses a ring of half a million ultrasonic emitters to build a sub-millimeter 3D body map in under 60 seconds. Built with Butterfly Network under an exclusive ultrasound-on-chip license, the machine targets MRI-quality imaging at roughly 100x the speed, with company-run scanning spas planned over the next 12 months.

How&How gives Brunel's SS Great Britain a second life as Bristol Dockyards (1 minute read)

How&How rebranded Brunel's SS Great Britain as the Bristol Dockyards, expanding the single ship into a three-part cultural destination spanning the Being Brunel museum, the Brunel Institute archive, and the vessel itself. Zaha Hadid Architects completed the Songshan Lake Exhibition and Performance Center, a waterfront complex of three interconnected volumes whose curved forms reference traditional Cantonese opera costumes. Sited in Guangdong's Greater Bay Area, the building anchors an undulating 1,200-seat Grand Theatre alongside exhibition space, extending ZHA's signature parametric language into a civic cultural venue for south China.

The 2026 Core77 Design Awards Toys &amp; Play Winners (2 minute read)

Jury lead Irene Guerrieri said selections prioritized quality of human interaction and inclusivity, weighing play as a fundamental part of life at every age.

The golden rule of Customizable Select (5 minute read)

Customizable Select arrives in Safari 27, letting developers fully style native `<select>` elements (custom arrows, icons, color swatches, option layouts) without JavaScript libraries or nested `<div>` hacks, while keeping built-in keyboard navigation and accessibility. WebKit's one rule: always give option elements real text content or accessible labels, since icon-only options break screen readers, scannability, and unsupported browsers all at once.

Vibe Coding for Visualization Implementation: An Empirical Study of Practices and Challenges (1 minute read)

A study of 16 participants using AI "vibe coding" tools to build data visualizations found that natural-language generation lowers the entry barrier but struggles to align user intent with precise visual output. The authors map how practitioners prompt, evaluate, and iterate, and document where the loop breaks down when a chart needs exact encoding rather than an approximate result.
