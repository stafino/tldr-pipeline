Apple currently has no senior design role, and Ternus has reportedly spent significant time with the industrial design group ahead of his September 1 start and a fall foldable iPhone launch.

Atonemo and NTS launch Radio Player with icon wheel for genre selection (1 minute read)

Atonemo and NTS released the Atonemo NTS Radio Player, a pocket-sized wireless streamer that plugs into any speaker and uses a physical icon wheel to pick genres rather than a screen or search field. The design leans on "omakase listening," handing curation to NTS hosts instead of the listener, a deliberate counter to algorithmic feeds and infinite-scroll music interfaces.
