David Kohn Architects designs London penthouse to resemble "house on a hill" (1 minute read)

David Kohn Architects completed House on a Hill, a three-storey penthouse atop a Victorian warehouse in Covent Garden, treating the rooftop as a new ground plane with stepped bay windows and a planted terrace. The southern living spaces (dining room, library, and lounge) run as a doorless enfilade opening onto the garden, framing central London skyline views from a retreat positioned at the city's center.

What are hypertokens? The layer between tokens and components, rebuilt for agents (3 minute read)

Jake Albaugh of Figma coined "hypertokens" at Config 2026 to name the layer between single-decision tokens and full components: a bundle of related decisions like a heading's family, size, weight, and line height, defined once so machines read intent instead of guessing it. The argument is that AI speeds execution but not clarity, so design-system drift that humans once smoothed over by eye now compounds when agents build exactly what they find.
