Free The Icons (5 minute read)

Rogue Amoeba's Paul Kafasis argues Apple should end MacOS 26 Tahoe's mandate that every app icon conform to a single squircle, a rule that shrinks noncompliant icons into gray-boxed "icon jail" and flattens decades of distinctive Mac icon shapes. He notes the uniformity also hurts usability, since identical shapes leave color as the only way to distinguish apps at a glance, which fails for anyone with color vision deficiency.
