Getty Images is canceling its $3.7 billion Shutterstock merger due to UK restrictions (1 minute read)

Getty Images terminated its $3.7B merger with Shutterstock after the UK's Competition and Markets Authority demanded Shutterstock divest its global editorial business, including its celebrity and news photo agencies, as a condition of approval. The collapse leaves both stock-photo giants to face AI image competition alone, and shows that US antitrust clearance no longer guarantees a cross-border deal survives regulators elsewhere.

Free The Icons (5 minute read)

Rogue Amoeba's Paul Kafasis argues Apple should end MacOS 26 Tahoe's mandate that every app icon conform to a single squircle, a rule that shrinks noncompliant icons into gray-boxed "icon jail" and flattens decades of distinctive Mac icon shapes. He notes the uniformity also hurts usability, since identical shapes leave color as the only way to distinguish apps at a glance, which fails for anyone with color vision deficiency.
