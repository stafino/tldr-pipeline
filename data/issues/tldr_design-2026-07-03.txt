BIG arranges circular Dymak HQ around stepped courtyard (1 minute read)

BIG completed a 2,800-square-metre circular headquarters for Danish material supplier Dymak in Odense, wrapping a mass-timber structure in a Möbius-strip roof around an open-air stepped courtyard. The building overlooks Glisholm Lake and organizes offices and showrooms around the central void, with BIG citing low-carbon construction as the design's guiding constraint.

Wordgard: In-browser rich-text editor from the creator of ProseMirror (1 minute read)

Marijn Haverbeke, creator of ProseMirror, released Wordgard, an in-browser rich-text editor library built around a semantic content model rather than free-form HTML. Teams define exactly which content types their editor supports and drive it through a programming interface aimed at complex, demanding editors, positioning Wordgard as a foundation layer rather than a drop-in widget.
