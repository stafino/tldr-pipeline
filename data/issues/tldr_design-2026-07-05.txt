This new typeface is perfect for a society that's done with shouting (1 minute read)

Type designer Jamie Clarke released Reel, a condensed high-impact typeface built around a Flexi-case system that lets designers mix capitals and lowercase within a single word or headline while keeping the tight rectangular structure that makes condensed type work. Developed with researcher Doug Wilson, it targets branding and editorial work where all-caps has started to read as aggression rather than authority, giving headline type a way to command attention without shouting.
