This clever rebrand finds its entire personality in one tiny flourish (1 minute read)

Lark Design Studio rebranded Wottz, a British maker of custom-built EV charging cables, around a single detail: a lightning bolt tucked into the crossbar of the double "tt" in the wordmark. The system pairs the existing core blue with an ownable neon yellow and sets it in Centra No.1, a contemporary nod to Gill Sans and Johnston that signals restraint over the category's usual clinical safe-mode palettes.

LMTLS creates wooden "gorge" inside New York skincare store (1 minute read)

LMTLS Architecture carved a steep canyon of stacked wooden layers through SKIN1004's new Soho flagship, its second store for the Korean skincare brand after Seoul. The design works the two-storey building's long, narrow footprint into a gorge-like path that pulls shoppers deeper into the space, a retail-as-landscape approach that treats circulation itself as the product display.
