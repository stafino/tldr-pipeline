Canva Code 2.0 just made vibe coding way less intimidating for everyone (1 minute read)

Users can start from a prompt, 50-plus templates, or imported HTML, then edit collaboratively in real time and publish to a custom or Canva-hosted domain.

'The world is deserving of better design': Koto's Jowey Roden on his mission to make quality typefaces affordable (1 minute read)

Koto, the branding agency behind identity work for Amazon, Google, Netflix, and WhatsApp, launched CcType, its first commercial font foundry, with typefaces priced for freelancers and small studios rather than enterprise clients. Co-founder Jowey Roden frames the foundry as an extension of the studio's Seasoned learning hub, applying the same access-widening instinct to a paid product built to put a decade of type craft within reach of smaller budgets.

Decibel's Circular-Material 3D Printed Chair (1 minute read)

Decibel 3D-printed a chair from Filaflex, a bio-based compostable filament from Italian materials company Balena, using robotic large-format additive manufacturing that eliminates molds and builds structural ribbing through controlled deposition. The wicker-like piece, which won a SIT Furniture Design Award, argues that production-ready furniture can skip fossil-based plastics without giving up sculptural form or everyday durability.

Designing Agent-Ready Websites for AI Web Agents: A Framework for Machine Readability, Actionability, and Decision Reliability (1 minute read)

This paper introduces the "agent-ready website," a design framework that restructures e-commerce pages around machine readability, action cues, and decision-reliability signals so AI browser agents can extract, compare, and complete purchases. In a 300-run test across three agent models, the agent-ready prototype hit an 89.3% strict success rate versus 49.3% for the human-oriented baseline, while cutting average steps from 9.31 to 6.49.

I tried Maxon's free After Effects alternative, and I don't want to go back (1 minute read)

Maxon released Autograph, a free GPU-powered motion graphics and compositing tool positioned as an After Effects alternative, spanning both motion design/animation and VFX/compositing workflows. The interface leans closer to a 3D package than a 2D design app, so After Effects users face a learning curve, but the timeline-based layer editing will feel familiar and the perpetual-free pricing sidesteps Adobe's subscription lock-in.

GPT-5.6 is Now Available in Figma Make (5 minute read)

Figma added OpenAI's GPT-5.6 as a model option in Figma Make, its prompt-to-prototype builder, citing faster and higher-fidelity first-pass output on complex UIs. The model self-heals on build errors rather than stalling, and stays faithful to existing Figma Design files, carrying over layout, spacing, styling, and working interactions like playback controls when generating from a spec.

What 32 design leaders do when told to move faster (1 minute read)

A survey of 32 design leaders finds that AI has collapsed the generation phase of design work, cutting production time from days to hours and prompting companies to demand ever-shorter timelines in return. The author argues the response is not to keep accelerating but to shift the value of the role toward judgment and problem framing, the parts that speed cannot compress.
