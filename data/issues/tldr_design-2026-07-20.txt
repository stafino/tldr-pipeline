I watched Lightcraft turn a screenplay into a virtual film set using an iPhone (1 minute read)

Lightcraft announced Spark Story at SIGGRAPH 2026, a screenplay-first previs platform that turns an imported Final Draft or Fountain script into 3D scenes built from Gaussian Splats, USD assets, or a library of 300-plus props and 25-plus environments. The pitch is shared pre-production alignment: a director frames shots by walking a virtual set through the iPhone AR app while collaborators watch the camera move live in a browser.

Coca-Cola gets new look that makes it even "more Coca-Cola" (1 minute read)

Coca-Cola redesigned its global identity with agency Jones Knowles Ritchie, modernizing the red-and-white palette, Dynamic Ribbon, Arden Square, and Spencerian script under a brief to make the brand "more Coca-Cola." The work prioritizes consistency across 200 markets and a flexible system built to hold up across packaging, retail, digital, and equipment rather than introducing anything new.

Jelly UI: Soft-body physics for native HTML form controls (1 minute read)

Jelly UI is a dependency-free Web Components library that wraps native HTML form controls in soft-body physics, shipping with dark mode, right-to-left support, and WCAG AA color tokens. Because it builds on real form elements rather than custom widgets, the tactile animation comes without sacrificing accessibility or keyboard behavior, though the squishy motion will read as too playful for most enterprise interfaces.
