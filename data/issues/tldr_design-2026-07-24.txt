SANAA proposal for National Museum of Ecuador selected after public backlash (1 minute read)

SANAA won the commission to design Ecuador's National Museum in Quito with a curved brick structure, replacing an earlier winning scheme that locals had criticised. The building will sit along the edge of La Carolina Park near work by BIG and Kengo Kuma, extending the studio's low, horizontal massing into a masonry vocabulary rather than its usual glass and white steel.

Flux 3 X Mimic: The Next Generation of Video-Action Models (1 minute read)

Black Forest Labs shared an early version of FLUX 3, a multimodal foundation model jointly trained on images, video, and audio, and used it to build FLUX-mimic, a video-action model that mimic robotics has deployed on robots tested at Audi. Video prediction consumed over 95% of training compute, and the team frames action control as one more low-dimensional view of the same physical reality the model already learns from video.
