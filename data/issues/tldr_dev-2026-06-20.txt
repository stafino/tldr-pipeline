AURpocalypse now: a look at the recent AUR attacks (1 minute read)

Attackers spun up new Arch User Repository accounts to adopt orphaned packages and push malicious updates that installed malware on users' systems. The AUR has no review process for new packages or updates and roughly 14,000 of its 107,000 packages are orphaned, so maintainers spent days manually pulling compromised packages and have now suspended new-user registration while they weigh a longer-term fix.
