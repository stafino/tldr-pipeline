Public PoC Released for Critical libssh2 CVE-2026-55200 Client-Side SSH Flaw (1 minute read)

A public proof-of-concept landed for CVE-2026-55200, a critical libssh2 flaw (CVSS 9.2) where a malicious SSH server triggers an integer overflow in ssh2_transport_read() and an out-of-bounds heap write on the connecting client, with no credentials or interaction required. Because libssh2 is embedded and often statically linked into curl, Git, PHP, backup agents, and firmware updaters, a distro package bump will not patch the copies you do not know are there.
