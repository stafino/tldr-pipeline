Wayfinder Router: deterministic routing of queries between local and hosted LLM (1 minute read)

Wayfinder Router decides whether each prompt should go to your small local model or your big cloud one by reading its structure and wording, running offline in microseconds with no API call, classifier, or LLM judge. Cheap prompts stay local while hard ones hit the expensive model, and the decision is deterministic and free, though semantic-only difficulty has no structural tell and lexical cues ship off by default.
