The design layers eBPF packet filtering, GENEVE overlay networks, and distributed egress proxies, plus an EDT-based eBPF bandwidth limiter and dual-tier policy enforcement to handle connection limits and port exhaustion. It runs across all Snowflake regions, supporting petabyte-scale transfers and latency-sensitive integrations.
