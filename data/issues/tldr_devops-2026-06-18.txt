Exploring Statistical Change Point Detection Techniques for Performance Anomaly Detection at Mozilla (1 minute read)

Mozilla engineers benchmarked 25 change-point detection methods and 15 ensemble approaches against Perfherder, the Student's T-test system that flags performance regressions across hundreds of daily commits and currently produces 12.5% false positives while missing 6.8% of real regressions. Validated against a ground-truth dataset of 174 time series annotated by eleven performance engineers, ensemble voting strategies improved F1-score by 11%, trading the precision-recall tug-of-war for more consistent detection.

10Gb/s Ethernet: switching to a Broadcom SFP+ module (1 minute read)

A 10GBASE-T SFP+ module in the author's study switch was overheating to 93C and thermal-flapping, shutting down at 95C and dropping the LAN until air conditioning kept it cool. The fix was swapping the Marvell-based MikroTik S+RJ10 for a Broadcom BCM84891-based 10Gtek module, which draws roughly 1.6W versus the older generation's higher power draw. The takeaway for anyone running 10GBASE-T over copper: check the PHY chip, since the Broadcom generation runs cool enough to avoid 24/7 cooling as a workaround.

Build and Deploy a Remote MCP Server to GKE in 30 Minutes (2 minute read)

The walkthrough covers the full path from a Python uv project to a secured public endpoint in roughly 30 minutes.

Why cloud native belongs at the heart of agentic AI: Lessons from building a multi-agent security platform on Kubernetes (1 minute read)
