Shipping huggingface_hub every week with AI, open tools, and a human in the loop (5 minute read)

Hugging Face documented how it moved huggingface_hub from a release every 4-6 weeks to a weekly release driven by a single GitHub Actions workflow. The team automated the mechanical steps (version bumps, tagging, downstream test branches, post-release PRs) and used open-weights models to draft release notes and announcements, keeping a human in the loop only where judgment matters. The whole setup runs on open-source tools with no vendor contract or closed model required.
