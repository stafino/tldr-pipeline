Innovations

Kraken launches crypto perps trading in the US on Kraken Pro (1 minute read)

Kraken launched perpetual futures trading for US customers on Kraken Pro, enabled by its May acquisition of Bitnomial, a CFTC-licensed exchange, clearinghouse, and brokerage. The launch gives Kraken a regulated path to offer crypto perps domestically, a product US traders previously accessed mostly through offshore venues operating outside CFTC oversight.

Sui Processes $65 Billion in Stablecoin Transfers in Five Days After Zeroing Out Fees (1 minute read)

Sui processed nearly $65B in stablecoin transfers since June 10 after zeroing out gas fees for stablecoin movement, the Layer 1's gasless design absorbing transaction costs at the protocol level. The shift recasts Sui as a fee-free settlement rail aimed at institutional and agentic payments, where per-transaction cost has been a barrier to high-volume, machine-initiated flows.

Trends

Canadian payments company Nuvei agrees to acquire NY-based cross-border payments company Payoneer for ~$2.75B in cash; the deal is expected to close in mid-2027 (Reuters) (1 minute read)

Nuvei agreed to acquire cross-border payments firm Payoneer for ~$2.75B in cash, with the deal expected to close in mid-2027. The acquisition pairs Nuvei's merchant-acquiring and global payout infrastructure with Payoneer's cross-border account network serving SMBs and marketplace sellers, consolidating two players competing for international settlement flows.

Bernstein says Robinhood could see ‘strong tailwinds’ as World Cup drives record prediction market volumes (1 minute read)

Bernstein projects Robinhood's prediction market revenue will climb from $150M in 2025 to $586M in 2026, citing record event-contract volumes driven by the World Cup. The analysts see strong tailwinds for the category as Robinhood expands beyond equities and crypto into prediction markets, a segment now contributing materially to its trading revenue mix.
