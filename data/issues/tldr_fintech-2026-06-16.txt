Sui Processes $65 Billion in Stablecoin Transfers in Five Days After Zeroing Out Fees (1 minute read)

Sui processed nearly $65B in stablecoin transfers since June 10 after zeroing out gas fees for stablecoin movement, the Layer 1's gasless design absorbing transaction costs at the protocol level. The shift recasts Sui as a fee-free settlement rail aimed at institutional and agentic payments, where per-transaction cost has been a barrier to high-volume, machine-initiated flows.

Deep Dives & Analysis

Invisible Manipulation Channels in AI-Assisted Financial Advisory: Implications for Market Integrity and Regulatory Design (1 minute read)

This paper identifies a manipulation channel at the sampling layer of LLM inference that lets adversaries systematically bias AI-generated credit and investment opinions while passing output-based audits, including statistical watermarking. The authors show detection is statistically hard, since the KL divergence between manipulated and normal output distributions can be driven arbitrarily small, forcing impractically large sample sizes. For regulators relying on output audits to police AI advisory, the implication is that integrity controls must move into the inference pipeline itself.
