An AI Security Agent for Banking: Multi-Vector Fraud and AML Detection Across Retail and Corporate Accounts (1 minute read)

This paper presents an AI security agent for retail and corporate banking that runs parallel transaction and session streams, each fusing an LSTM behavioral model, a velocity monitor, and a graph module tracking fan-in/fan-out and pass-through ratios for AML detection. On a synthetic log of 237,669 transactions across 13 threat categories, it hit F1 of 0.787 and 0.867, beating rule-based (0.562/0.733) and LSTM-only baselines, with Critical-tier response under 0.43 ms.
