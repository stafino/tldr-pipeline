Build Your Own Transaction Foundation Model for Financial Intelligence (1 minute read)

NVIDIA published a developer walkthrough for building a transaction foundation model end-to-end, using transformer pretraining on unlabeled transaction sequences to replace brittle hand-engineered features across fraud detection, credit scoring, and segmentation. The example reproduces a near-50% lift in average precision over a strong XGBoost baseline on the IBM TabFormer fraud dataset, following the same approach behind Stripe, Nubank, Visa, and Mastercard production models.

SEC Move Could Bolster Tokenized Stocks (1 minute read)

SEC Commissioner Paul Atkins signaled the agency may revisit Rule 611, the order-protection rule that governs how trades route to the best available price, in a move that could clear regulatory friction for tokenized stocks. Loosening Rule 611 would make it easier for blockchain-based equity venues to operate without routing to traditional exchanges, potentially opening a path for tokenized securities to trade at scale in US markets.

Senate, House reach agreement on housing bill banning CBDC through 2030 (1 minute read)

House Republicans attached the anti-CBDC provision to the must-pass housing bill, aligning with the Trump administration's stance that a US central bank digital currency is off the table.
