GoMining challenges Jack Dorsey's Square with payments system designed around bitcoin (5 minute read)

CFTC, SEC request public comment to clarify definition of ‘swaps’ amid CME lawsuit (1 minute read)

The CFTC and SEC jointly requested public comment on how to define "swaps," after CME Group sued the CFTC for classifying perpetual futures as futures contracts rather than swaps. The classification carries real stakes for venues like CME, since swaps fall under stricter clearing, margin, and reporting regimes that would reshape how perpetuals can be listed and traded in US markets.
