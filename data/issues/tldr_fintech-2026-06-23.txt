Credit Line on UPI: How India Bolted a Lending Rail Onto a Payments Network (3 minute read)

In September 2023, the RBI extended UPI so a pre-sanctioned bank or NBFC credit line can sit behind a UPI handle and be drawn against at the QR code, identical to a normal payment. This bolts underwriting, exposure limits, and interest accrual onto a rail built to debit funded accounts in real time. RuPay hit 16% of India's credit card spend in 2025, nearly half of it routed through UPI rather than a card swipe.
