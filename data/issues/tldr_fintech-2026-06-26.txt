Spain says ‘no exceptions or extensions’ for Binance, other crypto firms ahead of MiCA deadline (1 minute read)

Spain's securities regulator, the CNMV, said it will grant no exceptions or extensions to Binance and other crypto firms that miss the December 30 deadline to obtain MiCA authorization. Firms operating without a license after the cutoff must halt services to Spanish customers, raising the stakes for exchanges still navigating the EU's harmonized licensing regime across member states.

Bipartisan senators call for CFTC investigation into Polymarket after fake bets report (1 minute read)

Bipartisan senators John Curtis and Adam Schiff asked the CFTC to investigate Polymarket after a Wall Street Journal report found the $15B prediction market paid creators to stage roughly $1.9M in fake winning bets across its platform. The letter to Chair Michael Selig questions whether the agency is equipped to police prediction markets, escalating regulatory risk for a sector the CFTC is already reportedly probing.

Duffy’s Last Dance (1 minute read)

Net Interest profiles Terry Duffy, CME's longtime chairman, whose climb from the lean hog pit to running a derivatives powerhouse trading 28.1 million contracts a day now faces its biggest threat: perpetual futures, the crypto-native contracts that never expire.
