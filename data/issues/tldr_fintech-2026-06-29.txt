Europe’s unlicensed crypto firms face ‘wipeout’ as MiCA deadline hits (5 minute read)

ESMA called on unauthorized crypto-asset service providers to wind down in an orderly manner as MiCA's transitional period ends July 1, when pre-MiCA national registrations expire across the EEA. Only 244 firms hold MiCA licenses against more than 3,000 registered VASPs in 2024, and OKX Europe's CEO estimates 80% of crypto players won't survive the combined MiCA, EMI, and payment-institution compliance burden.

BIS warns stablecoins are more like ETFs than actual money, and they're creating FX risk (5 minute read)

The Bank for International Settlements argued in its annual report that stablecoins behave more like ETF shares than money, citing secondary prices that drift from par and redemptions that aren't guaranteed to settle instantly at face value. The BIS also warned that dollar-pegged stablecoins are accelerating dollarization in vulnerable economies, undermining local currencies, evading capital controls, and creating FX risk without ever settling on central bank balance sheets.

India’s USDT premium tops 8.5% as crypto remittance crackdown squeezes stablecoin supply: report (1 minute read)

The crackdown targets a roughly two-year-old workaround in which NRIs used USDT to bypass FEMA documentation requirements, and lands days before Parliament's finance committee meets the RBI on July 2 to weigh virtual-asset rules.
