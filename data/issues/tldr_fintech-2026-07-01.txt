Europe is rewriting its landmark crypto rulebook MiCA as hard July 1 deadline passes (5 minute read)

The EU's transitional grandfathering period under MiCA ended July 1, forcing crypto-asset service providers without full licensing to halt operations in the bloc, even as the European Commission opens a consultation on updating the framework. The review targets stablecoins, which have become central to payments since MiCA was drafted in 2020-2023, and now look under-served compared to newer regimes like the US GENIUS Act.

AI, Forging New Risks, Isn’t a Market Wizard After All (1 minute read)

Wall Street researchers warn that widely adopted AI trading models could make hedge fund strategies riskier rather than smarter, because when firms deploy the same tools they converge on correlated trades. That crowding amplifies volatility and raises the odds of synchronized selloffs, echoing the herding dynamics behind past quant blowups. The studies push back on the idea that AI is a market-beating edge, framing it instead as a source of systemic fragility regulators have yet to price in.
