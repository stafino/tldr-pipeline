Prediction Markets Swell to 27% of Sports Bets During World Cup (1 minute read)

Prediction markets captured 27% of US sports-betting volume during the World Cup, growing far faster than traditional sportsbooks over the same stretch. Kalshi and its peers operate as CFTC-regulated exchanges rather than state-licensed books, which lets them take national volume without 50-state licensing and leaves incumbents like DraftKings and FanDuel competing against a lighter regulatory cost base.

South Korea Details Plan for Foreigners to Trade Won Easier (1 minute read)

South Korea published a detailed plan to let foreigners trade the won freely, its furthest move yet toward full convertibility after decades of tightly controlled onshore FX access. Offshore market makers and payment providers would gain direct won liquidity outside Seoul trading hours, cutting hedging costs and settlement friction for cross-border flows into a market that still routes most non-deliverable forward volume through offshore proxies.
