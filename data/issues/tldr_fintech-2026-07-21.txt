The program replaces new sign-ups to iPhone Upgrade and arrives as memory-chip shortages push Apple hardware prices up, making financing a lever to protect unit volume.

Analyzing the evidence that helps businesses win “product not received” disputes (1 minute read)

Stripe analyzed evidence packets from one million disputes over 16 weeks and found that "product not received" claims submitted with delivery confirmation, a GPS carrier scan map, and a recipient signature won at a 44 percentage point higher rate than those without. Delivery confirmation alone accounted for 27 points of that lift, and timing mattered: evidence submitted after delivery was confirmed beat evidence showing only that a package had shipped. The gap persists because shipping data and dispute workflows sit in separate systems, making order-to-dispute matching manual.
