BIS warns USD stablecoins can evade capital controls, challenging traditional market regulations (1 minute read)

The BIS published a study of stablecoin flows across more than 130 economies finding that dollar-backed stablecoins are "largely unaffected" by broad or targeted capital flow restrictions, because much of the volume circulates outside the regulatory perimeter. Emerging-market central banks now have a dollarization channel their FX controls cannot reach, with USD stablecoin supply at $292.6B, up from $253B a year ago.
