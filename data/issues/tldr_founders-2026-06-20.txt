Lightfield Just Assembled a Working CRM Live On Stage, Then Unstuck a Stalled Deal in 3 Minutes (1 minute read)

Lightfield demoed an AI-native CRM that assembles itself from four connected sources: mail, calendar, a data warehouse, and a call recorder. No custom fields, no implementation project. Accounts arrived pre-enriched and opportunities were created automatically off emails and calls. In the demo, the system diagnosed a stalled enterprise deal by running code against every closed-won and closed-lost record, surfaced the pattern that won deals all had IT involved early, then found the CIO and drafted an intro email in about three minutes. Lightfield is now at roughly 3,000 customers.
