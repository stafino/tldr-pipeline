10x Coding Speed Won't 10x Revenue (1 minute read)

Faster code doesn't translate into faster revenue, because product success has always been about the market, not engineering speed. If AI lets you build 100 products where you once built 10, the win count stays flat at around 4, dropping the success ratio from 4-in-10 to 4-in-100. Software budgets aren't growing 10x and competitors are using the same tools, so the real bottleneck moves to go-to-market.
