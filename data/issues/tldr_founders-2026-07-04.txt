If Nothing Else – Segment Churn.  You’ll See Patterns and Learnings You Wouldn’t Otherwise (1 minute read)

Churn has no GAAP definition, so companies define it however flatters them, which is why segmenting your own churn matters more than any headline number. Split it by deal size: solopreneurs and sub-$99/month plans churn around 3% monthly as cards expire and businesses fold, mid-market credit-card deals hold near 100% net revenue retention, and $10k-plus enterprise deals run around 120%. Blend those together and you attack different problems with the same answer, because keeping a Fortune 500 account happy is nothing like stopping a small business from leaving for a cheaper tool.
