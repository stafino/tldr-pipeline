Federal contractors must comply with NIST PQC standards by the end of 2030, making cryptographic inventory and migration a near-term procurement requirement rather than a long-horizon planning exercise.

What 20 years of AWS taught me about agentic AI (3 minute read)

Spec-driven development keeps AI coding agents from wandering, argues a 20-year AWS engineer: instead of dropping an agent into a repo with a vague prompt, co-create a requirements spec, design document, and task breakdown in Markdown first. The implication for IT leaders is that agentic coding scales to critical codebases only with the same governance discipline as human teams, not autonomy. Acceptance criteria and strict testing become the contract that defines "done.
