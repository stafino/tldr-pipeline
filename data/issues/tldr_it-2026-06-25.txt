EU says AWS and Microsoft Azure should be treated as DMA gatekeepers (1 minute read)

The European Commission informed Amazon and Microsoft of its preliminary view that AWS and Azure should be designated gatekeepers under the Digital Markets Act, citing entrenched user bases, high switching costs, and customer lock-in. If the finding is confirmed, both vendors get six months to comply with DMA rules on interoperability and data portability, which could ease egress costs and multi-cloud exits for European enterprise buyers.

Windows 10 quietly gets one more year of support and updates (1 minute read)

Microsoft extended its consumer Extended Security Updates program for Windows 10 through October 12, 2027, pushing the security-only support deadline a full year past the previously announced 2026 cutoff. The quiet change spares enterprises and the hundreds of millions of devices still running Windows 10 from an immediate forced migration to Windows 11 or new hardware, easing near-term upgrade and procurement pressure.
