ITを揺るがす「エンタープライズ価格大転換」の波 (1 minute read)

The seat-based SaaS pricing model is breaking down as vendors shift toward usage- and outcome-based billing, moving cost-prediction risk from vendors onto buyers. Gartner projects that by 2030, at least 40% of enterprise SaaS spend will run on consumption, agent, or outcome billing, cutting seat-based revenue share from 21% to 15%. The practical takeaway for CIOs is to treat tokens like early cloud spend, stand up FinOps now, and pilot new pricing before any full rollout.
