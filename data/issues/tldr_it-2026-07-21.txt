The audit trail CIOs need before the next cyber crisis (2 minute read)

Green dashboards that mask legacy debt, undocumented service accounts, and shadow cloud instances become evidence against you once regulators and defense counsel start reconstructing the timeline. The recommended output is a documented audit trail of how risks were escalated and decided.

Generosity Under Conditions: Hardening Google Cloud Access Management (2 minute read)
