Electronic Arts launches EA Advertising, letting brands integrate directly into gameplay through dynamic, real-time placements, including stadium signage (Assiatou Hann/CNBC) (1 minute read)

Electronic Arts launched EA Advertising, an in-game ad platform that places brands into gameplay through dynamic, real-time formats like stadium signage across its sports and entertainment titles. The move opens EA's player base to advertisers seeking contextual placements inside live game environments, putting it in direct competition with Roblox, Activision, and other publishers building ad businesses around their audiences.

OptinMonster WordPress plugin hacked in CDN supply-chain attack (1 minute read)

Three Awesome Motive WordPress plugins, OptinMonster, TrustPulse, and PushEngage, were compromised in a supply-chain attack on the company's CDN, exposing sites that rely on them for email capture, social proof, and push notifications. Marketing teams running these tools should audit installs, rotate API keys, and review CDN-loaded scripts, since the affected plugins sit directly in the lead-capture and conversion stack.
