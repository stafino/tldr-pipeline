The Strategy That Grew Our AI Share of Voice by 35% (1 minute read)

Brainlabs grew its AI Share of Voice 35% and Mention Rate 42% by treating LLM visibility as four interlocking layers: content built around the prompts buyers actually type, distribution, earned authority, and ongoing maintenance. The biggest lever is off-site: roughly 85% of AI citations come from earned coverage, not your own pages, so PR and third-party mentions deserve more budget than most brands give them. The fastest wins, though, are on-site refreshes and internal linking, and a citation report shows which sources LLMs pull from in your category.
