Meta launches AI Mode in Facebook search to answer questions (1 minute read)

Meta launched AI Mode in Facebook Search, which uses Meta AI to answer questions directly with responses grounded in public posts, Groups, and Reels rather than a standard list of results. Meta hasn't said how AI Mode selects which content surfaces or whether brands and creators can see when their posts are used, leaving discovery and attribution on Facebook an open question for marketers.

Amazon Faces Billions in Penalties From Potential FTC Ad Suit (1 minute read)

The FTC is preparing a potential lawsuit against Amazon that could result in billions of dollars in civil penalties, alleging the company misled advertisers on its platform. The case targets Amazon's fast-growing ad business, now its third-largest revenue segment, and signals tighter regulatory scrutiny of how retail-media platforms disclose pricing, placement, and performance to the brands buying ads.
