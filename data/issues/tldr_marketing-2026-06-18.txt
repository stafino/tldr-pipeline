The UK's Competition and Markets Authority ordered Google to disclose how it ranks search results and to provide data portability to certain third-party services, part of its new strategic market status regime for general search. The ruling gives marketers a path toward more transparency on ranking signals and could pressure Google to open up the search data that has long stayed inside its black box.

The Australian Government to Require SMS/MMS Sender ID Registraion (1 minute read)

Australia's communications regulator ACMA is standing up a mandatory SMS Sender ID Register, requiring brands and senders to register alphanumeric IDs to combat scam texts that spoof legitimate businesses. Marketers running SMS campaigns into Australia will need to register their sender IDs to keep messages deliverable, adding a compliance step that mirrors the UK's existing registry and the carrier-level brand verification already standard in the US.
