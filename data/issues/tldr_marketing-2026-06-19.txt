AI Brand Visibility: You’re Tracking It Wrong (1 minute read)

Most AI brand visibility tracking just ports keyword-rank logic onto a system that doesn't produce ranks, treating prompts as the new keywords and visibility scores as the new rankings. The mismatch matters because LLMs are probabilistic: the same prompt yields a range of valid answers shaped by phrasing, context, and model version, so deterministic tools generate clean-looking data that misrepresents how the system behaves. Prompts like "Best CRM in 2026" also model a buyer with no context, history, or intent, meaning the fix is a different measurement philosophy, not better prompts.
