Snapchat launches ‘House of the Dragon’ AR ad in New York City (1 minute read)

Snapchat launched an interactive AR ad for HBO's "House of the Dragon" in Times Square, letting people project themselves onto large-format billboards and insert themselves into scenes from the show. The activation pairs Snapchat's AR tech with high-traffic out-of-home placement, giving brands a templated way to turn static billboards into shareable, camera-driven moments that extend reach into social.

Snapchat launches AI-powered ad creation tools (1 minute read)

Snapchat released a suite of AI-powered ad creation tools, including a chatbot-guided campaign setup and the Snap Creator Network that matches advertisers with relevant creator partners. The setup assistant lowers the barrier for smaller advertisers running Snap campaigns, while the creator-matching layer is Snap's bid to capture more influencer spend that currently flows to TikTok and Instagram.

Google adds AI bot to Ad Manager (1 minute read)

Google added a Gemini-powered AI assistant to Ad Manager that answers campaign questions in-stream, giving advertisers personalized guidance without leaving the dashboard. The move pushes routine campaign troubleshooting and optimization queries toward an AI layer, which could compress the time teams spend digging through Google Ads documentation and support to diagnose underperforming campaigns.
