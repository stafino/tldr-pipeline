Getty Images Strikes Deal with OpenAI, Sending Getty’s Stock Soaring (1 minute read)

Getty Images and OpenAI signed a multi-year deal to surface Getty's licensed visual content inside ChatGPT's search and discovery experiences, sending Getty's stock up more than 200% in premarket trading before settling around 123%. The terms stay vague on money, scope, and usage rights, but the move signals OpenAI is paying for licensed imagery to make AI answers more trustworthy after years of publishers fighting unlicensed training.

Amazon Prime Day Spending Expected to Rise 9% to $26.3 Billion (1 minute read)

Amazon Prime Day spending is expected to rise 9% to a record $26.3 billion during the 12th annual sale beginning Tuesday, with back-to-school clothes and household goods driving the bulk of online purchases. Higher gas prices are pushing deal-seeking shoppers toward the event, giving marketers a concentrated demand window to time promotions, retail media spend, and competing flash sales against.

Instagram looks to take on streaming services with longer-form, episodic and live formats for its TV app (1 minute read)

Instagram is expanding its TV app with longer-form video, episodic series, and live creator formats, positioning it against Netflix and Amazon Prime Video as it pushes into living-room viewing. The app is rolling out to Samsung TVs alongside existing Amazon Fire and Google TV support, and adding personalized channels, phone-to-TV casting, and horizontal video to capture creator audiences on the largest screen in the house.
