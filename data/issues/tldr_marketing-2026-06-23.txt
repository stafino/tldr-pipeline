Lighthouse agentic browsing scoring (5 minute read)

Chrome's Lighthouse added an Agentic Browsing category that runs deterministic audits on how well a site supports machine interaction, checking WebMCP tool registration and accessibility-tree integrity and reporting a fractional pass ratio rather than the usual 0-100 score. Marketers tracking AI-search and agent traffic now have a CI-friendly signal for whether their pages are structured for agents to read and act on, alongside the SEO checks they already run.

Adobe Just Made its Biggest AI Push Yet, and it Stretches from Photoshop to Disney World (5 minute read)

The strategy is to become the creative and marketing AI layer spanning solo Photoshop users to enterprise brands, with IP-clean models pitched as the wedge against scraped-data rivals like Canva and Figma.

What is a Good Organic CTR? Real Website Benchmarks (June 2026) (1 minute read)

Whole-site organic CTR now sits between 1% and 2% for most sites, based on aggregated Google Search Console data from 422,421 websites, with the median running well below the position-1 figures most teams cite. CTR is falling fastest in answer-heavy niches: Pets & Animals (0.80%) and Health (1.12%) trigger AI Overviews on 37% to 43% of queries. The takeaway for content teams is to shift effort away from informational keywords AI now answers and toward intent and formats Overviews rarely intercept.
