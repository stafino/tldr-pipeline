Google Ads updates target-based bidding for budget-limited campaigns (1 minute read)

Google Ads is changing how target-based bid strategies like Target CPA and Target ROAS behave under budget constraints starting August 17, pushing budget-limited campaigns to track their stated targets more closely instead of overdelivering. Expanding financial advertiser verification across Europe (1 minute read)

Google is expanding its financial services advertiser verification program to all 27 EU member states plus the EEA, adding 24 countries to a program already live in 18 markets and requiring financial advertisers to verify authorization against national regulators within 30 days or face restricted ads. The framework has already blocked or removed 327.8 million unauthorized financial ads globally, so finance marketers running Google Ads in Europe should budget verification lead time before launching new campaigns.
