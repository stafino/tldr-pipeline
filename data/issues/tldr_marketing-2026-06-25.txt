Keeping Data-Driven Content Fresh Was a Monthly Slog. So We Taught an Agent to Do It. (1 minute read)

Ahrefs built an internal agent, the Data Refresh Hub, to automate the monthly grind of updating its data-driven posts, pulling fresh numbers for 14 datasets from Keywords Explorer and Brand Radar, cleaning each by its own rules, and assembling a WordPress draft with new tables. The AI era requires a different kind of experimentation. (1 minute read)

The author argues that the experimentation playbook most teams built in 2022 is now obsolete, because faster AI-era product development makes the old emphasis on minor surface tweaks, two-week win cycles, and pre/post measurement instead of true A/B tests a poor fit.
