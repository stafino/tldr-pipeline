YouTube Shorts is getting 2x speed and a cleaner view, but it’s also ditching the dislike button (1 minute read)

YouTube rolled out four updates to Shorts, adding a Clear Screen mode, 2x playback speed, easier muting, and a viewing timer, while replacing the thumbs-up with a heart and removing the dislike button entirely. For marketers, losing the dislike signal cuts one of the clearest negative-feedback inputs on Shorts, pushing creators to read retention and "Not Interested" taps instead of vote ratios to gauge low-effort content.

Podcast Retargeting Outperforms Standard Campaigns by Up to 7x as YouTube Simulcasting Reaches New Highs (1 minute read)

Podcast retargeting campaigns drove a 7.4x higher visitor rate (0.52% vs 0.07%) and 5.5x higher purchase rate than standard podcast buys, according to Podscribe's Q2 2026 benchmark of 34B impressions across 98,000-plus campaigns. Even with lower incrementality (12% vs 24%), retargeting still produced roughly 2.75x more incremental conversions per impression. YouTube simulcasting hit 82% of episodic campaigns, and channels with all-U.S. subscribers redeemed promo codes at more than double the rate of channels with only 25% domestic reach.
