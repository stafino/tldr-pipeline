What is AI search optimization? (& why marketers should care) (1 minute read)

AI search optimization, which HubSpot calls answer engine optimization, is the practice of making a brand more likely to be cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and AI Overviews. The traffic is still small but converts well above search: across 1,200-plus publisher sites in a Microsoft Clarity study, AI-referred visitors signed up at roughly 11 times the rate of search visitors, and AI traffic grew 66% in 2025.

Adobe: online spending across all retailers in the US hit $26.4B during Amazon's four-day Prime Day event, up 9.3% YoY; Walmart and Target also hosted sales (Spencer Soper/Bloomberg) (1 minute read)

US online spending across all retailers reached $26.4B during Amazon's four-day Prime Day event, up 9.3% YoY, per Adobe, with Walmart and Target running competing sales. The expanded promo window pulls discretionary demand into a single July period, raising CPMs and CAC for any brand bidding on retail-media or paid-social during the event and rewarding teams that front-load creative and budget.
