FREE! The email subject line rules that don’t hold up! (1 minute read)

A University of Helsinki study of 31,812 subject lines sent 4.6 billion times found that classic "power words" like free, exclusive, and save actually lowered open rates, and ALL CAPS cut opens by about 3.3%. Shorter subject lines consistently won, personalization gave a modest lift, and a single exclamation point raised opens by nearly 4%. The takeaway for email teams is to A/B test the conventional advice against your own list rather than trusting inherited best practices.
