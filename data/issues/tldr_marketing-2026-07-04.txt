How to Get Your Developer Site Cited by AI Search Engines in 2026 (2 minute read)

AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews now handle roughly 12-18% of English informational queries, up from under 2% a year ago, and those visits convert at higher rates than traditional search. Generative Engine Optimization rewards the clearest structured answer to a specific question, not the highest-authority domain, so the tactics differ from SEO. The technical priorities: allow retrieval crawlers like OAI-SearchBot and PerplexityBot in robots.txt while optionally blocking training bots, and publish an llms.txt file of 20-50 priority links, as Stripe, Vercel, and Anthropic already do.
