Salesforce’s woes underline marketing’s agentic AI problems (1 minute read)

Only 34% of Salesforce customers have adopted Agentforce since its 2024 launch, and KeyBanc and Bernstein both downgraded the stock this month as roughly $200B in market value evaporated on slow uptake. Analysts blame fragmented CRM data and proof-of-concept-stage maturity, a signal that marketers eyeing agentic AI should fix data hygiene and connected systems before betting a campaign workflow on autonomous agents.

TikTok Strikes Multi-Year Global Partnership With NBA, WNBA (1 minute read)

TikTok signed a multi-year global content partnership with the NBA and WNBA, bundling creator access to league events with GamePlan tune-in tools and Pulse Premiere ad inventory tied to basketball content. The pitch for marketers is measurable feed-to-broadcast lift: fans are 42% more likely to watch a live game after seeing sports content on the platform, and the 2026 NBA Finals GamePlan features drove 497 million impressions.

The move advances Elon Musk's stated goal of full advertising automation, positioning X to build campaigns and persona matches against its 550M-user base without manual setup.

The Free Tools SEO Strategy: How to Rank With Calculators, Converters, and Generators (1 minute read)

Free tools like calculators, converters, and generators can rank for hundreds of keywords and hold traffic for years, and they hold up better against AI Overviews than how-to articles because AI can describe a calculation but can't hand users a working tool. Ahrefs grew its /writing-tools/ subfolder from zero to nearly 1M monthly US organic visits this way. With ChatGPT or Letaido cutting build time to an afternoon, the bottleneck is now picking which tool to build.

How Vanta’s $517K/month SEO Moat Translates Into AI Visibility (1 minute read)

Vanta has built one of B2B SaaS's strongest SEO moats by organizing content into topic-cluster subfolders around compliance frameworks like SOC 2 and ISO 27001, and that structure now translates into AI visibility: 2,603 of its 3,620 tracked keywords trigger Google AI Overviews, and it ranks top-three for 1,661 keywords, presence that would cost $517K a month to buy through paid search. The takeaway for demand-gen teams is that AI answer engines retrieve from existing content systems, internal linking, and third-party validation, so the brands winning citations aren't starting from scratch.

Stripe just bid $53.4B for PayPal. Here’s the part nobody’s pricing in. (1 minute read)

Stripe and Advent International bid roughly $53.4B for PayPal at $60.50 a share, a 28% premium, and analysts read the motive as a play for agentic commerce built on PayPal's ~440M active accounts and ~$1.8T in annual payment volume. For marketers, the signal is that AI agents are moving from researching purchases to completing them, so the brands an agent recommends win before a human compares options. G2's 2026 survey found 71% of B2B software buyers now use AI somewhere in the purchase, up from about 60% seven months earlier.
