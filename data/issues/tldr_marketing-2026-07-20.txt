The top content formats & types that earn AI search citations (1 minute read)

HubSpot's 2026 State of AEO Report found 58% of marketers are now optimizing content for answer engines, and the pages that actually earn citations share structural traits rather than topical ones. Question-led headings, self-contained direct-answer passages, schema markup, and internal linking that maps entity relationships are what let AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity extract and attribute a passage. The practical shift is writing sections that stand alone out of context, since extraction happens at the passage level, not the page level.
