Americans and AI 2026: Chatbots, Smart Devices, and Views on Impact (5 minute read)

What happens after coding is solved? | Fiona Fung (Manager of the Claude Code and Cowork Teams) (1 minute read)

Fiona Fung, who leads Anthropic's Claude Code and Cowork teams, argues that when AI handles most of the coding, the engineering manager's job shifts from coordinating output to deciding what's worth building and keeping context across parallel agent work. She reports Anthropic engineers now ship roughly 8x more code per quarter than in 2021, and names context-switching across concurrent AI tasks as the unsolved bottleneck no tooling has cracked yet.
