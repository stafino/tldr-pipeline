YouTube Shorts is getting 2x speed and a cleaner view, but it’s also ditching the dislike button (1 minute read)

YouTube rolled out four updates to Shorts, adding a Clear Screen mode, 2x playback speed, easier muting, and a self-limiting timer, while also swapping the thumbs-up for a heart and removing the dislike button entirely. Three of the four useful additions already exist in Instagram Reels, and dropping dislikes strips out one of the few signals viewers had to push back on low-effort content, leaving "Not Interested" and channel-level controls as the alternatives.

Notion kills its Gmail client after AI agents keep humans from troubling inbox (1 minute read)

Notion shut down its standalone Gmail client after finding that AI agents now triage and handle enough inbound mail that most users rarely opened the human-facing inbox. The move signals where assistant-heavy products are heading: when an agent does the routine work, the UI built for manual review becomes dead weight, and PMs should expect to justify every human-facing surface against an agent alternative.
