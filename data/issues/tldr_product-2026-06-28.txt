Instagram wants to make algorithm customisation a core part of the app, not a buried setting (1 minute read)

The move is a bet that discoverability drives adoption, though top comments on Mosseri's post show many users still want the simpler thing Instagram isn't building: a chronological feed of accounts they follow.

OpenAI Codex lead on the new shape of product work | Andrew Ambrosino (1 minute read)

In this interview, OpenAI's Codex desktop app lead Andrew Ambrosino argues that AI has inverted the product development process and made "taste" the most valuable PM skill, since anyone on the team can now build almost anything. He describes a "zone defense" model where PMs at OpenAI cover overlapping territory rather than owning fixed roles, and notes that nearly all OpenAI employees now use Codex weekly. The practical takeaway for PMs: as building gets cheap, judgment about what to build becomes the differentiator.
