10x Coding Speed Won't 10x Revenue (1 minute read)

The author argues that 10x faster coding won't translate into 10x revenue, because product success has always been gated by the market, not engineering speed. If teams ship 100 products where they used to ship 10, the same ~4 will win, dropping the success rate from 4-in-10 to roughly 4-in-100 since software budgets and the broader economy aren't growing 10x to match. The takeaway for PMs: cheap code shifts the real bottleneck to go-to-market, so prioritize finding real buyers with real pain over raw output.
