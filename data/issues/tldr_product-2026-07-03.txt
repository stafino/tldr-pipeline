TBM 428: Yes, Robot. Yes, Boss? (1 minute read)

John Cutler argues that AI amplifies whatever direction an organization is already moving rather than changing it. It can remove real drag when a behavior is blocked by workflow friction, missing scaffolding, or clerical burden, but it will not fix problems rooted in low psychological safety, misaligned incentives, or fear, and may make them worse by automating the existing avoidance and metric-weaponizing. The practical read for PMs: AI accelerates a team on the cusp of improvement and accelerates dysfunction just as easily.
