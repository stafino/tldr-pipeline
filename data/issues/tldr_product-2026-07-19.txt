TBM 431: The Denominator That Matters (1 minute read)

John Cutler proposes a multiplicative model for AI adoption: machine understanding times problem understanding times practice evolution, all over a denominator of organizational social contract. Any term at zero kills the product, so a team with deep domain expertise but no mental model of how the tools fail iterates fast in the wrong direction. His sharpest point is that resistance to AI mandates is rational rather than emotional, since improvement gains have no credible guarantee of not being captured against the people who generated them.
