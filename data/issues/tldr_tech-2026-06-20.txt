Where to Find the Colors Your Screen Can't Show You (1 minute read)

Most real-world cyans fall outside the gamut of standard RGB screens, which can only manipulate three cone types within a triangle that excludes the most saturated cyans, so a digital photo or game can never reproduce them. This post explains why human vision works through cone-cell response rather than wavelength, and shows how to find these unreproducible colors in the physical world without specialized equipment.
