Ultrasound imaging of the brain (1 minute read)

Aleph Neuro captured the most detailed vascular image of a living human brain to date, using ultrasound localization microscopy to see through an intact skull. The technique tracks blood flow as a proxy for neuron firing, reaching sub-millimeter resolution roughly 100 times finer by volume than comparable CT, a step toward non-invasive brain interfaces that avoid both skull-drilling electrodes and blurry EEG.
