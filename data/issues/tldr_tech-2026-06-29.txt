Obfuscation: building the final boss of cryptography (Part I) (5 minute read)

The Radiation Exposure Lie (1 minute read)

This Works in Progress essay argues that radiation regulation ignores a basic distinction between dose rate and total dose, treating small, spread-out exposures as if they were as dangerous as the acute doses that killed Chernobyl's first responders.
