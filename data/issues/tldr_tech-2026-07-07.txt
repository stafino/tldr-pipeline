Weak-to-Strong Generalization via Direct On-Policy Distillation (1 minute read)

This paper introduces Direct On-Policy Distillation (Direct-OPD), a method that reuses reinforcement learning gains from a small model to improve a larger one without rerunning expensive RL on the target.
