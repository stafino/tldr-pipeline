Apache Kafka performance #1 - linger.ms (5 minute read)

Kafka's default `linger.ms` change from 0 to 5ms, not any deep engine rewrite, explains the end-to-end latency shift Jack Vanlightly measured between versions 3.7.2 and 4.3.0. The batching config trades a few milliseconds of producer wait for larger, more efficient batches, and a rough formula (expected records per batch ≈ 1 + linger_seconds × per-producer-per-partition rate) shows why low-load workloads see higher latency while stressful ones get smoother, less spiky throughput.
